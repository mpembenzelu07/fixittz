Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your questions about our services

Je, naweza kupanga fundi kwa dharura? Can I book a handyman for emergency?

Ndiyo! Tuna huduma ya dharura inayoweza kukutana nawe ndani ya masaa 2. Huduma hii inapatikana kwa huduma za maji na umeme.

Yes! We have emergency service that can meet you within 2 hours. This service is available for water and electrical services.

Je, huduma zenu zina udhamini? Do your services have warranty?

Ndiyo, huduma zote zetu zina udhamini. Huduma za maji zina udhamini wa miezi 6, umeme miezi 12, na ujenzi miezi 24.

Yes, all our services have warranty. Plumbing services have 6 months warranty, electrical 12 months, and construction 24 months.

Je, naweza kupata makadirio ya bei kabla ya kazi? Can I get price estimate before work?

Ndiyo, fundi wetu atakupa makadirio sahihi ya bei kabla ya kuanza kazi yoyote. Huna malipo hadi makubaliano ya bei.

Yes, our handyman will give you accurate price estimate before starting any work. You don't pay until price agreement.

Je, wafundi wenu wana cheti? Are your handymen certified?

Ndiyo, wafundi wote wana cheti na usajili halali. Tunaweka wafundi wenye uzoefu wa miaka 3+ na rating ya angalau 4.5.

Yes, all handymen have certificates and valid registration. We list handymen with 3+ years experience and at least 4.5 rating.
