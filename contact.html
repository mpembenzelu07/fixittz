Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your common questions about our service

Unaweza kumtafuta fundi kwa kutumia sanduku la utafutaji kwenye ukurasa wetu wa nyumbani. Weka hudua unayohitaji, eneo lako, na utaona orodha ya wafundi wanaofanya kazi hiyo. Unaweza kusoma tathmini, kuona ratings, na kuangalia bei kabla ya kuchagua.

You can find a handyman using the search box on our homepage. Enter the service you need, your location, and you'll see a list of handymen who do that work. You can read reviews, see ratings, and check prices before choosing.

Wafundi wanaweka bei zao wenyewe kulingana na uzoefu wao, eneo la kazi, na ugumu wa kazi. Bei zinaonyeshwa kama "kutoka" (TZS 30,000+) na unaweza kujadili bei kamili na fundi kabla ya kukubaliana na kazi.

Handymen set their own prices based on their experience, job location, and job difficulty. Prices are shown as "from" (TZS 30,000+) and you can discuss the full price with the handyman before agreeing to the job.

Unaweza kulipa kupitia mfumo wetu salama kwa kutumia M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, au kadi ya benki. Unaweza pia kulipa mkononi kwa fundi baada ya kukubaliana na bei.

You can pay through our secure system using M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, or bank card. You can also pay cash to the handyman after agreeing on the price.

Bofya kitufe cha "Jiandikishe" kwenye menyu, jaza fomu ya usajili, na tuma hati zinazothibitisha ujuzi wako. Timu yetu itakukagua na kukukubali ndani ya siku 2 za kazi.

Click the "Register" button on the menu, fill out the registration form, and submit documents proving your skills. Our team will review and approve you within 2 working days.

Ndiyo, wafundi wengi wanatoa udhamini wa kazi kwa muda fulani (kawaida siku 30). Udhamini husajiliwa kwenye mfumo wetu na unaweza kuufuatilia kupitia akaunti yako.

Yes, most handymen provide work warranty for a certain period (usually 30 days). The warranty is registered in our system and you can track it through your account.

Baada ya kazi kukamilika, utapokea barua pepe au ujumbe wa SMS yenye kiungo cha kutoa tathmini. Unaweza pia kuingia kwenye akaunti yako na kuona orodha ya kazi zilizokamilika na kutuma tathmini.

After work is completed, you will receive an email or SMS with a link to provide a review. You can also log into your account and see the list of completed jobs and submit a review.
