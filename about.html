Our Mission & Vision

The foundation of everything we do

Dhamira Yetu

Kuwaunda uhusiano wa kuaminika kati ya wateza na wafundi wa Tanzania kwa kutoa jukwaa salama, la kina na lenye ufanisi. Tunataka kurahisisha upatikanaji wa huduma za ufundi na kuboresha ubora wa maisha.

Our Mission

To build a reliable relationship between customers and Tanzanian handymen by providing a secure, comprehensive and efficient platform. We aim to simplify access to handyman services and improve quality of life.

Maono Yetu

Kuwa jukwaa kuu la Afrika Mashariki la kuunganisha wateza na wafundi, kuboresha ubora wa huduma, kuunda fursa za ajira, na kuwa msukumo wa mageuzi ya kitaalamu katika sekta ya ufundi Tanzania.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform in East Africa connecting customers and handymen, improving service quality, creating job opportunities, and inspiring professional transformation in Tanzania's handyman sector.

Hadithi Yetu

FixitTZ ilianzishwa mwaka 2023 kwa lengo la kutatua changamoto ya upatikanaji wa mafundi wa kuaminika katika Tanzania. Baada ya kusikiliza maswali mengi kutoka kwa marafiki na familia kuhusu ugumu wa kupata fundi mzuri, tuliamua kuunda suluhisho.

Leo, tumekuwa jukwaa la kimsingi linalounganisha mamia ya wafundi wenye ujuzi na maelfu ya wateza waaminifu katika mikoa yote ya Tanzania.

Our Story

FixitTZ was founded in 2023 with the goal of solving the challenge of finding reliable handymen in Tanzania. After hearing numerous complaints from friends and family about the difficulty of finding good handymen, we decided to create a solution.

Today, we have become an essential platform connecting hundreds of skilled handymen with thousands of trusted customers across all regions of Tanzania.
