About FixitTZ

Connecting you with reliable handymen across all regions of Tanzania

Dhamira na Maono Yetu

Our Mission & Vision

Dhamira Yetu

Kuwaunda uhusiano wa kuaminika kati ya wateza na wafundi wa Tanzania kwa kutoa jukwaa salama, la kina na lenye ufanisi.

Our Mission

To build a reliable relationship between customers and Tanzanian handymen by providing a secure, comprehensive and efficient platform.

Maono Yetu

Kuwa jukwaa kuu la Afrika Mashariki la kuunganisha wateza na wafundi, kuboresha ubora wa huduma na kuunda ajira.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform in East Africa connecting customers and handymen, improving service quality and creating employment.
